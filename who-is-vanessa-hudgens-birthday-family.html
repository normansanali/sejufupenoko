Who Is Vanessa Hudgens?, Birthday, Family, Social Media

Who Is Vanessa Hudgens?, Birthday, Family, Social Media: Born on December 14, 1988, Vanessa Hudgens, officially known as Vanessa Anne Hudgens is an American actress and singer.

She developed a love for acting and singing at a younger age and has been consistent throughout her career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after acts.

Hudgens made her film debut in Thirteen but became a household name portraying Gabriella Montez in the High School Musical film series which brought her significant mainstream media success

The success of the first film led Hudgens to acquire a recording contract with Hollywood Records, with whom she released two studio albums, V (2006) and Identified (2008).

Since the release of her studio albums and the High School Musical franchise, Hudgens has focused on her acting career.

She appeared in a number of films including; Bandslam, Beastly, Sucker Punch, Journey 2: The Mysterious Island, Spring Breakers, Second Act, Bad Boys For Life, and Tick, Tick…Boom!.

Hudgens also starred in the Netflix Christmas movies; The Princess Switch (2018) and its sequels (2020 and 2021) and The Knight Before Christmas (2019), and co-produced the latter three.

She has won several awards including; Blackbird Vineyards Visionary Award (Napa Valley Film Festival), Trendsetter Award (Young Hollywood Awards), and Favorite Movie Actress (Kids’ Choice Awards), among others.

In 2022, Hudgens co-hosted the Met Gala in Manhattan.
