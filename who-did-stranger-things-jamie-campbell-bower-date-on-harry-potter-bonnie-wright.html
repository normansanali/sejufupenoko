Stranger Things Jamie Campbell Bower Dated This Harry Potter Star

September 2024 · 5 minute read

Jamie Campbell Bower is back in everyone’s hearts with his role in the newest season of Stranger Things. He plays the role of Vecna, but this is not the only iconic role Bower has played. Bower acted in the Twilight Saga as a Volturi vampire named Caius, first appearing in New Moon and later in Breaking Dawn Part 1 and Breaking Dawn Part 2. The actor also played a young Grindelwald in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 and was able to reprise his role in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.

Jamie Campbell Bower's part in the Harry Potter franchise gave him more than just an acting job, though. While on set, Bower became very close with actress Bonnie Wright. The two dated for several years, and there was even an engagement involved before they eventually split. Let’s get into exactly what went down between Jamie Campbell Bower and Bonnie Wright.

8 Who Is Bonnie Wright?

Bonnie Wright is a British actress best known for her role in the Harry Potter franchise. She played Ginny Weasley, the youngest child and only daughter of the Weasley family. Wright was only 10 years old when she first appeared in Harry Potter as it was her professional debut as an actress. She appeared in all eight films and fans were able to watch her grow up on the big screen.

Since the conclusion of the franchise, Wright has founded her own production company, BonBonLumiere. The company produces short films, including films Wright directs herself.

7 Bonnie Wright Is An Activist

Outside her tremendous acting and producing career, Bonnie Wright has made a name for herself as a spokesperson for environmentalism. In 2017, Wright boarded the ship Arctic Sunrise with Greenpeace to monitor and discuss plastic waste. Following the voyage, Wright wrote an article for Greenpeace, saying “Every piece of plastic I have ever used is still somewhere on this earth, and that’s terrifying.”

Wright is also very outspoken about rainforest preservation and children’s rights and protection. When J. K. Rowling, the writer and creator of Harry Potter, posted a series of tweets targeting the transgender community, Wright was quick to express her support of the community.

6 How Jamie Campbell Bower And Bonnie Wright Met In Harry Potter

As many actor-couples do, Jamie Campbell Bower and Bonnie Wright met on set. Wright had already been a cast member in the Harry Potter franchise since the beginning, she made Bower feel right at home despite his late addition to the cast. Though the couple were never in any scenes together, as Bower’s role was centered around flashbacks, the two quickly hit it off. Their chemistry on set was undeniable to those around them, so it was no surprise to their friends when they began dating in 2009.

5 When Jamie Campbell Bower And Bonnie Wright Started Dating

Their relationship was obvious to those on the Harry Potter set, but fans of the couple were not given any confirmation until 2010. Jamie Campbell Bower and Bonnie Wright were very private with their relationship, and they had been dating for several months before they decided to go public. At the BAFTAs in February 2010, the Harry Potter couple finally confirmed their relationship for fans. Wright told a reporter, “Yes, we’re dating. We’ve been seeing each other for a few months; it’s good.”

After going public, Bower and Wright no longer felt the need to hide their relationship, but they still remained relatively private. The couple seemed very happy with each other.

4 Were Jamie Campbell Bower And Bonnie Wright Engaged?

Just over a year after announcing their joyous relationship to the world, the couple disclosed that they were engaged in 2011! Harry Potter cast mates were stunned by how fast Jamie Campbell Bower and Bonnie Wright were moving, especially considering that Bower and Wright were only 21 and 19 respectively. A source close to the couple revealed to Daily Mail, “All he has told us is that he asked her to marry him and Bonnie said yes.”

Friends were still supportive, however, since they had seen Jamie “on the set just to watch Bonnie and spend time with her between takes.” Bower also stated that “they are completely in love” and that his relationship with Wright was “the real deal.”

3 Why Jamie Campbell Bower And Bonnie Wright Split

Unfortunately for Jamie Campbell Bower and Bonnie Wright, the magic they found on the set of Harry Potter did not last. Apparently, nothing was perfect for the Hollywood couple. Their engagement was called off some time in 2012, ending their 3-year relationship and breaking Harry Potter fans’ hearts. A source revealed to Us Weekly in July 2012 that “Bonnie and Jamie split a while ago.”

Like their relationship, Bower and Wright kept the details of their breakup private. However, it was revealed that the split “was amicable, but things had not been going right for some time. It’s sad.”

2 Who Else Jamie Campbell Bower Dated

Jamie Campbell Bower wasted no time moving on from Bonnie Wright. Soon after their split was made public, Bower was seen holding hands with Lily Collins. The actor must have a thing for co-stars, because he and Collins both starred in The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones and kindled their relationship on set.

Bower and Collins dated on and off for many years, giving fans whiplash. Collins has since moved on and is now married to Charlie McDowell.

1 Bonnie Wright Married Andrew Lococo

Lily Collins isn’t the only one to have moved on from heart-throb Jamie Campbell Bower. Bonnie Wright is also married! In March 2022, Wright married Andrew Lococo in a private ceremony. Wright and Lococo’s relationship has been very private, so no one really knows how long they’ve been together. The couple first shared pictures of their relationship on social media in late 2020, and they moved in together in February 2021.

Wright shared the news of their wedding on Instagram. “Yesterday was the best day of my life thanks to my husband!!”
