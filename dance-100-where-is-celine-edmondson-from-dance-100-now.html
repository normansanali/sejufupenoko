Where is Celine Edmondson from Dance 100 Now?

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Introduction

Dance 100, a Netflix competition where choreographers compete for a $100,000 prize and manage 100 of the world’s best dancers, has been a popular hit among audiences. Celine Edmondson is one of the choreographers who gained much attention from fans. Although she didn’t win, people are curious about her career and what she’s been up to.

Who is Celine Edmondson?

Celine Edmondson is a 28-year-old choreographer from Bridgeport, Connecticut. She started dancing at a young age and pursued her passion by earning a BFA in dance from the University of the Arts. She signed with the Clear Talent Group in New York and became a performer and choreographer for the Brooklynettes in 2015, the official dance team for the Brooklyn Nets basketball team. She worked her way up to be Co-Captain and has also performed with artists like Bebe Rexha, Meghan Trainor, DJ Khaled, and more. Celine recently became a brand ambassador for Black Pyramid and performed at the 2020 VMAs with Maluma.

Celine Edmondson in Dance 100

Celine made a memorable impression on the audience and judges in Dance 100. She passed the first two rounds with flying colors but stumbled in the third round, which put her in a dance-off with Janick Arseneau. She won the dance-off and made it to the semi-final, where she had to do two routines with 25 dancers. Although the judges thought she could have elevated the performances further, Celine still did not make it to the final round.

Where is Celine Edmondson Now?

Celine’s career is still thriving even after her stint in Dance 100. She teaches at Steps Broadway Conservatory, a New York dance program for 17-24-year-olds. Her bio on their webpage states that she’s also working on TV and film projects. She’s also affiliated with the BLOC, a talent agency that represents choreographers for several things, but film and TV, which shows that she’s eager to do more work on-screen.

Celine Edmondson’s Social Media Presence

Celine Edmondson is active on both Instagram and TikTok. Her Instagram handle is @_ohitsboom, where she has 17.6k followers and mostly posts videos of herself dancing. Her TikTok account is called ohitsboom, with 1805 followers and 5741 likes. She posts random videos with no specific topic.
