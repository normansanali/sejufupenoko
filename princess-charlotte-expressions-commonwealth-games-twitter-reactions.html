The Best Twitter Reactions to Princess Charlotte's Expressions at the Commonwealth Games

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Twitter's latest royal obsession has arrived in the form of Princess Charlotte and her extremely candid facial expressions.

The seven-year-old princess appeared today at the 2022 Commonwealth Games in Birmingham, England, joining dad Prince William and mom Duchess Kate. While there were plenty of picture-perfect photos of the young royal smiling dutifully alongside her parents, there also emerged a slew of snaps that show her acting like, well, a normal child. (Ah, royals, they're just like us.)

In the photos, Charlotte ranges from exuding uncontainable excitement over the games to downright contempt for the affair.

"Princess Charlotte is giving her brother Louis a run for his money with these expressions today," one Twitter user wrote, likely referring to Charlotte's four-year-old brother's behavior during the Platinum Jubilee. In particular, while he and his family joined Queen Elizabeth II on the balcony at Buckingham Palace, Louis was visibly irate with the loud noises being emitted from the Royal Air Force's special flypast.
