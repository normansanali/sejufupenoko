The question was criticized as unprofessional and inappropriate.

The next day, Parker wrote that the comment was “an attempt at humor” and not a malicious attack. Parker has not written for the Detroit News since and has not attended any press conferences since the incident, including the one Marinelli gave following his dismissal as head coach of the Lions. On January 6, 2009, the Detroit News announced that Parker had resigned from the newspaper the previous week.

On January 14, 2017, Barry was hired by the Los Angeles Rams as the team’s linebackers coach and Assistant Head Coach.
