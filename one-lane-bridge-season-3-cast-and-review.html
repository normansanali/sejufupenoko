One Lane Bridge season 3 cast includes Dominic Ona Ariki, Joel Tobeck, Phoebe McKellar, and Alison Bruce. The series is set to release on January 23, 2023 on Acorn TV.

The One Lane Bridge follows the life of Detective Ariki Davis in the New Zealand television drama series. The show is set in the made-up town of Akaroa. It centers on the detective’s difficulties while attempting to get in touch with his past and solve a string of murders that have shaken the sleepy hamlet.

The series’ first season made its debut on TVNZ in April 2020. The series is renowned for its captivating characters and gorgeous location photography. The show received mixed reviews as some said it failed to live up to the drama of its magnificent locale.

On the other hand, some called it a fascinating, evocative, supernatural-tinged detective drama with superb performances from well-assembled characters. The series is created by Pip Hall and Philip Smith. At the same time, Pip also serves as the writer of this supernatural crime drama. The executive producers of the series are Philip Smith and Kathleen Anderson. Great Southern and Television TVNZ are production companies.

One Lane Bridge Story Review

One Lane Bridge is a story about Queenstown, New Zealand. It surrounds the story of Detective Ariki Davis, of Maori descent. 

He relocated from Auckland and accepted the position primarily to work with Chief Stephen Tremaine, a police officer he admires. The town’s entrance is via a causeway with a One Lane Bridge. Every day Ariki crosses the bridge, he notices strange things that vanish immediately, like corpses on the rocks below the bridge, a wheelchair in the river, or a gecko. He doesn’t know if he sees the past or the future or if it is all in his head.

Ariki quickly discovers he is reawakening the town’s mysterious and spiritual past. Small-town secrets threaten the stability of the neighborhood, and Ariki is plagued by ominous prophecy.

One Lane Bridge distinguishes itself from other crime drama miniseries by incorporating Mori mysticism. His character has “second sight” or matakite, which means he sees glimpses of the deceased.

Ariki gets in trouble with the people and risks his career as he struggles to accept the inquiry and his spiritual gift. The story of the One Lane Bridge surrounds the happening on One Lane Bridge.
