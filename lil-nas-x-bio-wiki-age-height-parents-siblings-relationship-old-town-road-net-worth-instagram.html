Lil Nas X Biography | Wiki

Lil Nas X, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter, born and raised in Lithia Springs, Georgia, U.S. He came to international attention for his country rap single “Old Town Road”, which first achieved viral popularity on the micro-platform video-sharing app TikTok in early 2019, and was Diamond certified by November the same year.

The song reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 and remained there for nineteen weeks, the longest for any song since the chart debuted in 1958. In June 2019, Nas X came out as gay, the only artist ever to have done so while having a number-one record. “Old Town Road” earned him two MTV Video Music Awards including Song of the Year.

Nas X won two Grammy Awards, for Best Music Video and Best Pop Duo/Group Performance along with being nominated for a total of six, the most for any male in 2019. TIME placed him on their inaugural TIME 100 Next list. Nas X was also named by Forbes on its 2020 30 under 30 lists.

Lil Nas X Career

Nas X was interested in music since the earliest years and even attended music classes, but then left them in order “to remain cool in the eyes of his peers”. He attended Lithia Springs High School and then entered the University of West Georgia, but left it after a sophomore year.

Lil Nas is a big fan of Nicki Minaj. e created a fan page, devoted to her songs and personality, in social media. Soon he decided to record his tracks, too. In late 2018 he uploaded “Old Town Road” to SoundCloud and YouTube. That was his finest hour – the song became a massive hit.

Lil Nas X Old Town Road

On December 3, 2018, Nas X released the country rap song “Old Town Road”. He bought the beat for the song anonymously on an online store from Dutch producer YoungKio for $30;[54] it samples Nine Inch Nails’ track “34 Ghosts IV” from their sixth studio album Ghosts I–IV (2008)

Lil Nas X joined two different music genres in one – his “Old Town Road” belongs to country rap style. The idea to join such contradictory music directions was named as “revolutionary” by many popular music issues.

“Old Town Road” topped the charts for 17 weeks in a row, thus having set a new record. The song has become the most popular within the last 60 years. Previously the record-breaking compositions were performed by Mariah Carey and Boyz II Men in 1995 and by Justin Bieber, Luis Fonsi, and Daddy Yankee in 2017.
