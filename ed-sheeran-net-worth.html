Ed Sheeran Biography: Age, height, birthplace, parents, siblings, education, awards

Edward Christopher Sheeran was born on February 17, 1991 in Halifax, England, and grew up in Framlingham. His father was John Sheeran(a lecturer), while his mother was Imogen, a cultural publicist who later became a jewelry designer.

He has an older brother named Matthew Sheeran(music composer). While attending Thomas Mills High School, he began creating his own songs and was later admitted into the National Youth Theatre.

He has a height of 5 feet 7 inches (1.71 meters or 171 centimeters). Sheeran is a 30-year old star now.
