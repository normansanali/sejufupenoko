Two Executives Unite: Jason Bolton and Oliver Wiener Join David Tyree's Podcast

July 2024 · 2 minute read
Recently, Jason Bolton, the CEO of Fan Arch, and Oliver Wiener, its COO, were invited onto The Catch the Moment Podcast hosted by NFL champion David Tyree. The trio discussed a variety of topics, from their own entrepreneurial journeys to their love for sports to Jason’s incredible story as a cancer survivor. With David’s help, these two shared their experience and perspective on the challenges and rewards of running a successful business.

At the start of the podcast, David welcomed Jason and Oliver to the show and began the conversation by asking them to share their story. Jason provided an overview of how he and Oliver had met, and how they decided to join forces to create Fan Arch. He also discussed how his own life-altering experience with cancer had impacted his outlook and approach to business.

The podcast then shifted to discussing sports and the impact they have had on the lives of both Jason and Oliver. Oliver shared his passion for sports and how it had inspired him to become an entrepreneur. Jason discussed his passion for football and how it had motivated him to push through the difficult times during his battle with cancer.

David Tyree is a former American football wide receiver who played for the New York Giants from 2003 to 2008. He is most known for his iconic catch in Super Bowl XLII during the Giants' victory over the New England Patriots. The catch, which came on a deep pass from Eli Manning, is widely considered one of the most remarkable feats in NFL history.
