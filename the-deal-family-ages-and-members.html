The Deal Family Members and Ages

The Deal Family ages are Brandon Deal (32), Meagan Deal (31), Mckenzie Deal (12) and Sarah Deal (6). They are from Dothan, Alabama.

The Deal Family Members are Brandon, Meagan, and two daughters.

In an interview with News4, the family of four opened up about topics that will help viewers understand the Deal family even more.

The Deals of Geneva may seem like a normal family, but there is more to know about them. Their videos are available on TikTok, which will give an insight about them.

During the time of the interview, Kenzi was 10 years old and loved Barbies, and wished to be a nurse someday. She is deaf and tries to educate others about deaf people through videos.

The family lost their son, James Mitchell, who was stillborn on April 17, 2021. However, they visit his grave quite often.

The reason the family started making such videos was explained by Brandon in the interview, “In one of the videos, her processor was showing, we got a question about it, so we answered with another video, and it just snowballed from there.”

People’s curiosity turned into a medium of expression for the Deal family.

The other member of the Deal family includes Mia, Kenzi’s service dog. She has passed her obedience training and her service training hopefully.

Meet The Deal Family on Tiktok

The Deal Family on Tiktok is quite popular with 2.5M followers. Their Tiktok likes have reached 110.3 million.

The videos they post are basically common family content and layout awareness for those suffering from hearing loss.

They have managed to become a place for solutions and relaxation for others with the help of their videos.

One of the tiktok became viral in the summer of 2020 with 5 million views.

Some of the videos other than comedy are McKenzi based. They are basically awareness videos like;

  • How my deaf daughter listens to her music
  • Lake safety with a deaf daughter
  • What Kenzi takes to the lake
  • Dancing with a cochlear implant
  • Advice for migraines

Brandon created a TikTok set to Dua Lipa’s “Blow Your Mind.”

The family’s tiktok account has earned 10M views on a video where The Deal family collaborated with Walmart+.

Brandon Deal net worth is estimated at around $100,000. His earning comes from the video he makes on TikTok.

While most of their videos are comedy based, one part of the daily life they mostly share about is co-sleeping. The YouTube Video explains it.

They all sleep together in one bed. The reason is mostly linked with McKenzi, who turned deaf at the age of six.

Meagan explained that she was too worried for her daughter to be alone, not even across the room. She used to place her hand on McKeanzi’s body the whole night, to let her feel the presence.

The sleeping arrangement just got stagnant, and then Sarah was born. The parents thought that they would still feel better with them staying, and therefore, the sleeping arrangement never changed.

The couple highlighted that there is no reason for Sarah sleeping together, only that they love keeping their daughters close.

One of the reasons they co-sleep was highlighted in November 2021 Tiktok video. Here we can see the father-daughter duo discussing her processor and where she keeps it at night.

She usually takes it at night and keeps it in charge. This seems like a sound sleep for some but might be scary for McKenzi since she won’t be able to hear anything.

McKenzi herself doesn’t feel safe sleeping alone, as explained by her in her Tiktok video.

Brandon further explained in another video that if any alarms were to go off or an emergency was to happen, McKenzie would have no idea about it.

That is why co-sleeping helps to have his daughter’s back in need.
