Aleksandr Robak is well known to the public as a Russian entertainer and producer. His full name is Aleksandr Removich Robak. He stands at 6feet 1inches (186cm) tall and has a black hair color.

He was born on December 28, 1973, in Zlatoust, Russia. His nationality is Russian and he holds Capricorn as his birth sign.

Early Life, and Family

He was born and raised in Zlatoust, Russia by his parents. He has got lovely and supportive family members. Ram Aleksandrovich Robak is his father who is a designer by profession and Raisa Lukinichna is his mother who used to be a teacher in a private school. From his early education, he was interested in playing guitar. 

Education

He did not face any difficulties in his childhood days for quality education and for a good life because of his strong family background. 

He is well educated and finished his early education at Yaroslavl Theater Institute. After that Aleksandr Robak graduated with a Bachelor's degree from US state university. However, he has not disclosed any sort of information regarding his majors.

Professional life and Career

He started his career from scratch and has done more than 50 films in his career. He came into popularity after he portrayed his role as Leonid in 'To Lake 2' which is a TV series. 

He has been part of many hit movies and TV series such as Christmas Trees 8 as Biznesmen, Honest Divorce as Gena, Insomnia as Pavel, Lucky Trouble as Botsman, Naydyonysh 2 as Georgiy, and many more.
