Download Call of Duty for Windows - Free

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Call of Duty is one of the most influential shooters in gaming history. It changed the whole industry for good. Find out why.

Aim and fire!

Get in the boots of an American, British or Russian soldier and travel back in time to the battlegrounds.

The first game made by Infinity Ward, the original studio that was later acquired by Activision, is a milestone in the first person shooter industry. When it first launched, back in 2003, turned into an instant classic.

Jumping into this game you might notice the shaky graphics, however it is still a very enjoyable game for modern standards. .

Over ten hours of pure gameplay experience without any filler will make you feel like you’re really fighting for your life. No matter how many World War II first-person shooters of its kind and era exists: once you play Call of Duty you know there’s no competition.

Call of Duty features an impressive AI that makes you feel like you’re never fighting alone. But beware, your foes also share the same capacity. You will find quite the challenge facing off against the opponents that the campaign provides.

The multiplayer mode finds countless ways to keep you entertained. Its attention to detail is much appreciated by the community. Log in and fight other players through a variety of modes like deathmatch and team deathmatch.

Where can you run this program?

You can run this game on a Windows or Mac OS X. If you’ve got a Windows 7, or higher, you’re covered.

Is there a better alternative?

No. Even though many people tend to lean more to the Medal of Honor side, Call of Duty features a hefty load of great additions that are unique to the franchise.

Our take

Call of Duty is the cornerstone of a genre that keeps on growing. It remains an exciting choice of gaming according to modern standards.

Should you download it?

Yes, you definitely should. No matter if you played back in 2003, or it’s your first time: Call of Duty remains a very fun game.
