Cardi B &quot;She Bad&quot; Lyrics

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Last night, Chrissy Teigen was one of the many Cardi B fans reacting to the rapper's debut album, Invasion of Privacy. Unlike other listeners, however, Teigen received a personal shoutout in the lyrics. On the track "She Bad," featuring west coast rapper YG, the ever-outspoken Cardi B alluded to having a ménage à trois with Teigen and Bad Gal Riri:

"I need Chrissy Teigen
Know a bad bitch when I see one (yeah, woo)
Tell Rih-Rih I need a threesome"

Naturally, the shoutout took Teigen by surprise. Around the time the record dropped, the TV host, Twitter star, and cookbook author was happily making some biscuits in the kitchen:
