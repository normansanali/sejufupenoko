Jamie Laing Biography

Jamie Laing is a British actor, entrepreneur, and social media influencer who was born and brought up in Oxford, England. He rose to fame for his role in the award-winning television series ‘Made in Chelsea.’ The series aired on the UK’s channel ‘E4,’. He joined the cast in the second season of the series. Jamie is also one of the two presenters of the podcast, ‘Private Parts.’ 

Laing also did a cameo in 2017 as a doctor in an episode of the soap opera ‘Hollyoaks.’ Jamie has also appeared in a few reality series such as ‘Celebrity Hunted,’ ‘I’m a Celebrity: Extra Camp,’ and ‘The Great British Bake Off.’ Of these, the last one was a debacle for Jamie.

Jamie Laing Candy Kittens

Laing’s huge fondness for candies led him to set-up a candy company. He felt that the market had very little variety to offer. He also felt that the quality of the candies was also not up to the mark. During his university days, he gave his business idea the final thought.

He combined three of his loves- candies, fashion, and girls to create the sweet-treat company; ‘Candy Kittens.’ This confectionery line specializes in making gluten-free gummy sweets infused with unusual flavors. All their products are claimed to be made from real fruit juices. The company later launched a few exotic fruit and vegetable flavors.

The use of natural ingredients, authentic flavors, and attractive packaging made the company unique. Jamie runs the business along with two of his friends namely Ed Williams and Warren Crane.
